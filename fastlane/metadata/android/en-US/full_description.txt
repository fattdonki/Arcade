<h1>Arcade</h1>
<p>Arcade is a minimal, Material You-themed game launcher. It allows you to block offline games from accessing the internet using a VPN. This saves battery, blocks ads, and protects your privacy.</p>
<h2>Features</h2>
<ul>
<li>Distraction free launcher letting you focus on your library, with clean Material design</li>
<li>Block selected games from accessing the network</li>
<li>Rename games</li>
<li>Automatically enable or disable Do Not Disturb for selected games</li>
</ul>
<h2>Permissions</h2>
<p>Arcade requires DND permissions, notification permissions, and VPN permissions.</p>
